## Icon Button

`IconButton` is built on top of MUI's [IconButton API](https://mui.com/api/icon-button).
Like a `Button`, it uses `ButtonBase` under the hood. Similar to `text` buttons, it also accepts
a `bg` prop to determine the background mode ("light" or "dark"), which affects its styles.
